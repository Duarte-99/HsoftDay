Sobre mi

WebDevBlog is a blog aimed at Web Developers interested in building wonderful websites. It has been created in 2019 by Jean-Baptiste Jung, a French web developer, blogger and web enthusiast. Jean has made websites for over 15 years and started blogging in 2007. Before WebDevBlog, he founded CatsWhoCode and WPRecipes, and has been writing for popular tech blogs Smashing Magazine and Dzone. He also wrote the WordPress Cookbook, published by Packt Publishing. WebDevBlog is also on Twitter and CodePen.
